Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story revolves around Dom Cobb, a professional thief who specializes in infiltrating the subconscious of his targets to steal valuable secrets. Given a chance to have his criminal history erased, Cobb must perform the impossible task of "inception" — planting an idea into someone’s mind. The film explores complex themes of dreams, reality, and the human psyche, all wrapped in an action-packed narrative.

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with a talented ensemble cast including Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, Cillian Murphy, and Marion Cotillard.
